My Favorite Recipe: Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings

I don't really have a favorite recipe but I really love Garlic-Parm Wings so here is a Recipe from Delish.com(but with ranch dreessing>:D)

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. Chicken wings (with bone)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • 4 Minced garlic cloves
  • 3/4 cup finely grated Parmesan (can add more if desired)
  • 1/2 cup Melted unsalted butter
  • 2 Tbsp. Chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp. Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Ranch for dip (as much as you want>:D)

Procedure

  1. Arrange a rack in center of oven; preheat to 400°. Set a wire rack in a large rimmed baking sheet. In a large bowl, toss wings with oil; season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Transfer wings to prepared rack and bake until golden and crispy, about 50 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, stir garlic, Parmesan, butter, parsley, and red pepper flakes. Add wings and toss until coated.
  4. Transfer wings to a platter. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Serve with a side of Ranch! ! ! ! !.
